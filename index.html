About Us

Welcome to Serenity Haven Hotel, your oasis of luxury and comfort in the heart of Nairobi. Since 2005, we have been dedicated to providing exceptional service and a memorable experience for every guest. Our elegantly designed rooms, gourmet dining options, and top-notch amenities ensure a perfect stay, whether you're here for relaxation or adventure. Discover the perfect blend of tranquility and convenience at Serenity Haven Hotel. We look forward to welcoming you.

Our Rooms

PEACE OF MIND

Discover peace of mind in our Tranquility Room, designed to provide the ultimate sanctuary from the outside world. This elegantly appointed space features soothing décor, plush bedding, and modern amenities, creating an environment perfect for relaxation and rejuvenation. Whether you're unwinding after a long day or seeking a quiet retreat, the Tranquility Room offers a serene haven where you can truly relax and recharge.

DELUXE ROOM

Experience the epitome of comfort in our Deluxe Room. Thoughtfully designed with luxurious furnishings and modern amenities, this spacious room offers a perfect blend of elegance and convenience. Enjoy a restful night's sleep on plush bedding, unwind in the cozy seating area, and take in stunning views from your private balcony. The Deluxe Room promises an indulgent stay, ensuring you feel pampered and refreshed.

COZY ROOM

Welcome to our Cozy Room, a warm and inviting retreat designed for ultimate comfort. This charming space features soft, plush bedding, intimate lighting, and thoughtfully selected furnishings that create a homely atmosphere. Perfect for solo travelers or couples, the Cozy Room provides a serene and restful environment where you can unwind and feel at ease. Enjoy a peaceful stay with all the modern amenities you need in our Cozy Room.
